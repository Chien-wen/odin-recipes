Pineapple fish

Pineapple fish is a dish my mother taught me. It has the umami taste of fish and the sweet and sour taste of pickled pineapple. It is rich in flavor and very delicious.

Ingredients

  • Pickled Pineapple Sauce
  • Ginger
  • Tilapia
  • oil
  • water
  • salt（optional）

Instructions

  1. 爆香炒酱：
    热油爆香姜片，加入适量凤梨酱，炒制香味散发
  2. 下鱼炖煮：
    放入罗非鱼，加入适量水炖煮15min后收汁，可视情况加盐调味
