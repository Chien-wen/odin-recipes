Flavored egg cake

Flavored egg cake cross section of cake

My girlfriend likes cakes very much. The thick texture and mellow egg flavor are her favorites.

Ingredients

  • 40g Low-gluten flour
  • 4 eggs(60g for each)
  • 40g sugar
  • 40g oilt
  • 40g milk or water
  • 3ml lemon juice or vinegar
  • 6 inch square baking pan

Instructions

  1. 混合面糊：
    加热油，混合蛋黄、面粉、牛奶
  2. 蛋白打发：
    蛋白加入醋，开始打发至小弯钩，期间糖分3次加入
  3. 混合入模：
    均匀混合面糊与蛋白
  4. 烤箱开烤：
    180度烤箱预热15min，蛋糕出炉后从30CM高处将烤盘摔在桌面震出底部热气
